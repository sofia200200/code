Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. — Juicy, tender chicken infused with zesty lemon and aromatic herbs, ready in under an hour.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 50 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 1.5 kg / 3.3 lb)
  • 2 lemons, one halved, one sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 small onion, quartered (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels — this helps the skin get crispy.
  3. Season the cavity with salt and pepper, then stuff with the halved lemon and quartered onion (if using).
  4. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, chopped rosemary, thyme, crushed garlic, salt, and pepper.
  5. Rub the herb-oil mixture all over the chicken, under the skin, and over the legs.
  6. Place the chicken in a roasting pan. Arrange lemon slices on top and around the chicken.
  7. Roast for 35–45 minutes, or until the juices run clear and the internal temperature reaches 74°C (165°F).
  8. Rest the chicken for 10 minutes before carving. Spoon pan juices over the sliced meat.
